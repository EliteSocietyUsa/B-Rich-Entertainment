About Us

B-Rich Entertainment is an independent record label based in Boston, Ma. Our objective is to provide Boston and eventually New England area artists a place to come ang get their music heard. We will provide a starting point and be a stepping stone for artits looking to secure major record label deals. We will assist artist by providing studio time, promotion, mixing and engineering services, cd duplication, marketing and advertising, and local distribution. Through these efforts also hope to help clean up the streets of Boston by providing promotional concerts and by giving local kids a chance to be involved with our street team.
